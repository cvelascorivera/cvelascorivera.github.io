Carlos Velasco Rivera

I am a PhD Candidate in Politics at Princeton University. I am also currently a graduate student fellow at the department's Program for Quantitative and Analytical Political Science (Q-APS).

My research interests include: political dynasties in democracies and autocracies; the electoral returns of programmatic politics; structural estimation; and political economy
